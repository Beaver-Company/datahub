What is DataHub?

DataHub is a (GitHub-like) hosted platform for organizing, managing, sharing, collaborating, and making sense of data.

It provides an efficient platform and easy to use tools/interfaces for:

  • Publishing of your own data (hosting, sharing, collaboration)
  • Using otherâ€™s data (querying, linking)
  • Making sense of data (analysis, visualization)
