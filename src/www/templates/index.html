A Data Ecosystem for Individuals, Teams and People


What is DataHub?

For End Users:

  • A way to store your data centrally, without having to set up your own database
  • A way to collaborate with others
  • A way to seamlessly share your data with friends and colleagues
  • A suite of tools to process your data

For Developers:

  • A database-agnostic, language-agnostic platform for your mobile and web apps
  • A web client for easy manipulation of your data
  • A application ecosystem for data processing, including ingestion, curation, integration, discovery, query, analytics, visualization, and machine learning
  • An open source project from MIT CSAIL's Living Lab
